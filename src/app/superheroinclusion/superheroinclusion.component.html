Growth
There is a miracle in every new beginning
Childhood is the most beautiful out of all of life's seasons
Youth is the pollen that blows through the sky and does not ask why
Adulthood is not an age but a stage of knowledge of self
Middle age is youth without levity and age without decay
Old age is a record of one's whole life
Wrinkles mean you have laughed, grey hair mean you have cared, scars mean you have lived
The journey isn't over yet...
